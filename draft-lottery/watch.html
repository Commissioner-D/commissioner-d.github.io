Draft Lottery

Teams without a self-selected draft spot enter this lottery and are randomly assigned one of the remaining FIFA World Cup nations. The draft order is then filled from the back forward: the nation eliminated first fills the last available pick, while the nation that stays in the tournament the longest gets the earliest available pick. If two lottery nations are eliminated in the same round, the exact time of elimination decides the order. For the final four, results decide the order directly: the third-place match sets positions 3 and 4, the final sets positions 1 and 2, with each match’s winner picking before its loser.
